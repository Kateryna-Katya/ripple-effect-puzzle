How to Play

Objective

Fill all empty cells with numbers based on two core rules

Rooms:

Each bold-lined Room must contain consecutive numbers starting from 1. A Room with 3 cells must include 1, 2, and 3

No Duplicates:

In rows and columns, repeated numbers must be separated by a distance equal to or greater than the number itself. E.g., two "2"s in a row must be at least two cells apart.

Strategy

Think ahead, plan placements, and use logic to eliminate possibilities

Use Hints

Tap the lightbulb icon if you're stuck – smart hints will guide without spoiling

Advance Through Levels

Solve puzzles to unlock new ones and track your progress
